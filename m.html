Top 5 Tamil Movies

Vikram (2022)

A high-octane action thriller starring Kamal Haasan, as a covert operative fighting a drug cartel.

Jailer (2023)

Rajinikanth delivers a powerful performance in this action-packed story of justice and redemption.

96 (2018)

A heartwarming romantic drama featuring Vijay Sethupathi and Trisha as high school sweethearts.

Master (2021)

Thalapathy Vijay stars as an alcoholic professor who takes on a juvenile reform school mafia.

Karnan (2021)

A gripping tale of a rebellious young man, played by Dhanush, who rises against injustice in his village.
