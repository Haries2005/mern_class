Viduthalai Part 1

"Viduthalai Part 1" follows the journey of Kumaresan, a newly recruited police constable who is assigned to assist a special task force operating in a remote, forested area. The task force is on a mission to capture "Perumal" alias Vaathiyar, the leader of a revolutionary group called "People's Army," which fights against the state's oppression of tribal villagers. As Kumaresan witnesses the brutality and human rights violations committed by the police in the name of national security, he begins to question his role in the system. He also develops a relationship with a village girl named Tamilarasi, which deepens his emotional connection to the villagers. The story explores themes of state violence, personal morality, and freedom, highlighting the stark contrast between the oppressed and those in power. The film ends on a tense note as Kumaresan slowly realizes the truth behind the government's actions and begins to sympathize with the rebels, setting the stage for the sequel.

Pottu

"Pottu" is a horror-thriller film directed by Vadivudaiyan. The story revolves around a medical college student named Bharath, who becomes possessed by the spirit of a transgender woman named Pottu. Initially, Bharath is a normal student, but after a series of supernatural events, his behavior changes drastically. The spirit of Pottu seeks revenge against those who wronged her in the past, especially targeting a corrupt and cruel doctor who once exploited her. As the story unfolds, we learn about Pottu’s painful backstory, how she faced injustice due to her identity, and how her soul now uses Bharath as a medium to expose and destroy the evildoers. The film mixes horror, social issues, and revenge with dramatic twists, ending with the message of justice for the marginalized.

Hero

"Hero" is an action-drama film directed by P. S. Mithran. The story follows Sakthi, a small-time fraud who makes fake educational certificates for struggling students. His life changes when he meets a young, brilliant girl named Mathi, whose dream is to become a scientist. However, the corrupt education system suppresses her innovation, and she tragically takes her own life. Deeply affected, Sakthi decides to fight against the system. Guided by a mysterious masked vigilante known as Sathyamoorthy (played by Arjun), Sakthi transforms into a real-life "hero" who exposes the truth and inspires children to follow their passions rather than blindly obey the flawed system. The movie blends social commentary with superhero elements, emphasizing the need for educational reform and celebrating creativity over rote learning.

>Sulthan

"Sulthan" is a Tamil action-drama film directed by Bakkiyaraj Kannan. The story centers on Vikram, also known as Sulthan, the son of a powerful don. Sulthan is a soft-hearted and educated man who returns home from abroad, only to find his father's 100-man gang—who raised him like a brother—still involved in violent activities. After his father's death, Sulthan decides to reform the gang and give them a peaceful, respectable life. Their journey takes them to a village under threat from corrupt businessmen who want to destroy the farmers' land. Sulthan and his men take a stand to protect the village, blending his peaceful ideals with necessary action. The film explores themes of loyalty, change, and redemption, showing how a man born into violence can choose a different path for himself and others.

Singam II

"Singam 2", directed by Hari, is a high-octane Tamil action film and a sequel to Singam (2010). The story follows Durai Singam, an honest and fearless police officer, who goes undercover as a school teacher in a coastal town to secretly investigate illegal drug trafficking. While the public thinks he's been transferred for disciplinary reasons, he’s actually on a secret mission assigned by the Home Minister. Singam discovers that an international drug cartel, led by a powerful smuggler named Danny and supported by local criminals like Thangaraj and Bhai, is using the coastal region as a hub. Facing both political pressure and personal risk, Singam fights through layers of corruption, deception, and violence. The movie combines intense action, patriotism, and emotion, with Singam ultimately bringing the criminals to justice and exposing the international drug syndicate.
